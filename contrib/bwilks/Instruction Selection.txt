IR -> Expand -> Simplify -> Match -> ASM

 ----------------
      EXPAND
 ----------------

 Generate Low Level IR (LLIR) from the input IR
 
 LLIR is a subset of normal IR.

 e.g. no lea (gets expanded to arithmetic on the pointers)


 ----------------
      SIMPLIFY
 ----------------

 Peephole optimiser on the LLIR


 ----------------
      MATCH
 ----------------

Compares the simplified LLIR against the pattern library
to generate machine code.
